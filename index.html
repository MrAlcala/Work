Fathom Oilfield Service, formaly known as Drurmight Oilwell Service, started out as a small well service company in the 1950's. Although ownership has changed throughout the years, Fathom remains a very strong presence in their original core area, and throughout Oklahoma. They have adapted to changes in the industry, both with equipment and personell. When operators started drilling deeper plays, Fathom resoponded by diversifying its fleet to include high horsepower, heavy hood load, double triple rigs to answer the needs of teh industry. Now that Resource plays have become a reality, Fathom is locating yards in strategic areas and moving in deeper equipment to handle the rigors of deep horizontal wells. Fathom Oilfield Service will continue to provide customers with the specific equipment and working alliances needed for their exploration efforts.
